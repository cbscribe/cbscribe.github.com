Teaching

My newest project is teaching kids to code using Python and the Raspberry Pi. I believe that a basic understanding of software engineering provides a set of fundamental skills that is vital, both to the child's future and the future of the global economy. It's not just about STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math), but also about liberal arts: being able to think critically about problems is the first step to finding solutions. An early introduction to computer science is an investment in the future.

My wife and I have started KidsCanCode - please check it out and contact us if you're interested in working with us!
